EUREKA

Eureka, a prestigious paper presentation competition, fosters innovation and excellence in research and communication. Participants delve into diverse topics, presenting insightful papers and engaging presentations. It's a platform for showcasing intellect, creativity, and scholarly achievements.

JIDNYASA

Jidnyasa is a project competition where teams compete by presenting their innovative projects. It fosters creativity, problem-solving, and teamwork, offering participants an opportunity to showcase their practical skills and solutions to real-world challenges.
